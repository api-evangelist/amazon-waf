  <p><span class="LinkEnhancement"><a class="Link" href="https://aws.amazon.com/" rel="noopener" target="_blank">Amazon Web Services (AWS)</a></span> is pleased to announce that the Winter 2025 System and Organization Controls (SOC) 1 report is now available. The report covers 184 services over the 12-month period from January 1, 2025 – December 31, 2025, giving customers a full year of assurance. This report demonstrates our continuous commitment to adhering to the heightened expectations of cloud service providers.</p> 
  <p>Customers can download the Winter 2025 SOC 1 report through AWS Artifact, a self-service portal for on-demand access to AWS compliance reports. Sign in to <span class="LinkEnhancement"><a class="Link" href="https://console.aws.amazon.com/artifact" rel="noopener" target="_blank">AWS Artifact in the AWS Management Console</a></span>, or learn more at <span class="LinkEnhancement"><a class="Link" href="https://aws.amazon.com/artifact/getting-started/" rel="noopener" target="_blank">Getting Started with AWS Artifact</a></span>.</p> 
  <p>AWS strives to continuously bring services into the scope of its compliance programs to help customers meet their architectural and regulatory needs. You can view the current list of services in scope on our <span class="LinkEnhancement"><a class="Link" href="https://aws.amazon.com/compliance/services-in-scope/" rel="noopener" target="_blank">Services in Scope page</a></span>. As an AWS customer, you can reach out to your AWS account team if you have any questions or feedback about SOC compliance.</p> 
  <p>To learn more about AWS compliance and security programs, see <span class="LinkEnhancement"><a class="Link" href="https://aws.amazon.com/compliance/programs/" rel="noopener" target="_blank">AWS Compliance Programs</a></span>. As always, we value feedback and questions; reach out to the AWS Compliance team through the <span class="LinkEnhancement"><a class="Link" href="https://pages.awscloud.com/compliance-contact-us.html" rel="noopener" target="_blank">Contact Us page</a></span>.</p>
