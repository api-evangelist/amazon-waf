As AI agents, bots, and programmatic access become an increasingly significant portion of web traffic, organizations need better tools to understand, analyze, and manage this activity. Today, we’re excited to announce AI Traffic Analysis dashboards for AWS WAF protection packs—also known as web access control lists (web ACLs)—providing comprehensive visibility into AI bot and agent behavior across your applications. The challenge: Understanding AI bot traffic The rapid proliferation of AI bots—from search engine crawlers to research agents—has fundamentally changed the nature of web traffic.
